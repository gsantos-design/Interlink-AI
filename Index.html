FAQ
A few of the questions people ask before they plug multiple models into one workflow.
Which models does Interlink AI support?
You bring your own API keys for the models you already use. Interlink AI acts as a thin orchestration layer on top, so you’re never locked into a single provider.
Is my data used to train any models?
No. Interlink AI is designed so that your prompts, logs, and routing policies stay within your control. Model training is governed by your agreements with each provider.
Can I expose this to my own customers?
Yes. The pricing plans on this page are compatible with Stripe Checkout, so you can sell your own Interlink AI workspace as part of a product or service.
